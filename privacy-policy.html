Privacy Policy for 1.000

Last updated: March 2026

1.000 ("the App") is a reflex timing game developed by RBapp. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. We do not require you to create an account, and we do not collect your name, email address, location, or any other personally identifiable information.

Advertising

The App displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. Google AdMob may collect and use data to show relevant ads. Please review Google's Privacy Policy for more information on how Google handles data for advertising purposes.

Data Safety

The App does not collect any user data. All game progress (scores, levels, streaks) is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to any server.

Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
